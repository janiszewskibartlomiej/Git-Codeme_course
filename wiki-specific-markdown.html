paragraph is simply one or more consecutive lines of text, separated by one or more blank lines.&#x000A;Line-breaks, or softreturns, are rendered if you end a line with two or more spaces:</p>&#x000A;&#x000A;<pre class="code highlight js-syntax-highlight plaintext" lang="plaintext" v-pre="true"><code><span id="LC1" class="line" lang="plaintext">Roses are red [followed by two or more spaces]</span>&#x000A;<span id="LC2" class="line" lang="plaintext">Violets are blue</span>&#x000A;<span id="LC3" class="line" lang="plaintext"></span>&#x000A;<span id="LC4" class="line" dir="auto">&#x000A;<a id="user-content-multiple-underscores-in-words" class="anchor" href="#multiple-underscores-in-words" aria-hidden="true"></a>Multiple underscores in words</h3>&#x000A;&#x000A;<blockquote dir="auto">&#x000A;<p>If this is not rendered correctly, see&#x000A;<a
